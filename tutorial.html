How to Play

Rare Fish Trade is a game about buying and selling (fake) fish in a highly volatile market. The game can be played for as long as you want; the goal is to earn as much in-game cash as possible. You start the game with no money, only a single fish.

Market

To earn more money, you need to watch the market page, which displays the prices of each type of fish in the game. The prices continually update at a regular interval, and can get better or worse as time goes on. View the different types of fish on the left by scrolling through their listings, and hit the trade button to sell fish you have or buy new ones. The graph in the right displays the prices of each fish over time. Most fish investors know the best way to make money is to sell their fish when the price is higher than usual, and use the earned cash to invest in a different fish that is at a lower price than usual. Smarter investors will watch the market for trends and use that information to make the wisest purchases.

Collection

The collection is the best way to view your current money and display your fish. You can click the facts button to learn interesting trivia about the fish you currently own.

Settings

You can change the speed and volatility of the market at any time for a more relaxed or intense experience.
